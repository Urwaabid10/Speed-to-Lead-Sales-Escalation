## Overview

In real estate, speed is critical. Studies show that if a lead is not contacted within five minutes, the probability of conversion drops significantly.

This project implements an automated speed-to-lead system that ensures every new inquiry is instantly acted upon through forced calls and escalation logic, eliminating delays and preventing lost opportunities.

---

## Problem Statement

* Agents take hours to respond to new leads
* Delayed response drastically reduces conversion rates
* Lack of accountability in lead follow-up
* High-value leads going cold

---

## Solution

An automated system that:

* Instantly notifies and connects agents with new leads
* Forces immediate action through automated calls
* Monitors lead status and escalates unattended leads
* Ensures no lead remains untouched

---

## Features

### 1. Instant Lead Trigger

* Workflow is triggered as soon as a new lead submits a form
* Captures lead data in CRM for immediate processing

---

### 2. Force-Call System

* Automatically calls the assigned agent
* Connects the agent directly with the lead
* Eliminates delays in manual dialing

---

### 3. Real-Time Lead Handling

* Leads are placed in a "New" stage upon entry
* Agents are expected to update lead status after contact

---

### 4. Escalation Logic

* If no action is taken within 15 minutes:

  * System sends an internal SMS notification to the Broker or Manager
  * Lead is moved to a "Hot/At-Risk" stage
* Ensures accountability and immediate follow-up

---

### 5. Pipeline Management

* Organized lead stages for visibility:

  * New
  * Contacted
  * Hot/At-Risk
* Enables better tracking and performance monitoring

---

## Workflow Summary

1. Lead submits inquiry form
2. Workflow is triggered instantly
3. System initiates a call to the assigned agent
4. Agent is connected with the lead in real time
5. System monitors lead status for 15 minutes
6. If no update:

   * Internal SMS is sent to Broker
   * Lead is moved to "Hot/At-Risk" stage
